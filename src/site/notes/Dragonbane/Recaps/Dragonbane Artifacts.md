# STATUETTE FRAGMENTS
- Key fragment #1 (base & feet) with Grim
- Key fragment #2 (head) with Arasin
- Fake Key fragment #2 (head) with Moby 

# FIENDCRUSHER
- A magnificent light warhammer set with jewels (mastercrafted with STR requirement 7 and durability 15). 
- Magical and glows red whenever the bearer is within 10 meters of a demon.
- With Grim

# GILDED CHAINMAIL
- The gilded chainmail is light and flexible (armor rating 4, only gives bane to SNEAKING rolls)
- With Grim

# DRAGON KNIGHT CROWN
- A gilded crown imbued with a spell which halves all damage from attacks by demons (rounded up). This is according to the runes on the crown.
- With Moby

# REDITUS & ANCORA STONES
- They are two rune-inscribed stones with the following inscriptions:
	- Stone 1: Reditus sum (“I have returned”)  
	- Stone 2: Ancora (“Anchor”)  
- When you hold the Reditus stone and shout “Reditus sum”, your stone instantly returns you to where the Ancora stone is.
- After teleporting, roll a D6. On a 1, the stones will crack and its power falters. If you roll a 1 again, it will work one last time and then crumble to dust.
- With Moby

# BOTHILD’S BROADAXE
- A mastercrafted battle axe Bothild’s Broadaxe (STR Requirement 10, durability 12), adorned with runes that recount its owner’s exploits.
- With Munin
